Mental Health and Well-being

Mental health is at least as important as physical health for a happy, meaningful life. Poor mental health can interfere with maintaining relationships, having a sense of satisfaction in one’s self and one’s work, and functioning in day-to-day life. Mental illnesses can dull or block even our basic interests in food, sleep, and sexual contact.
